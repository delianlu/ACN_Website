ABOUT

ACN is a multinational company with a focus on engineering, technical services, and innovation. The company employs a highly-skilled group of engineers with years of expertise and technical training that are competent in the majority of engineering disciplines.

ACN has created innovative solutions to real problems that our clients are facing. The engineering, procurement, and construction industries must be developed at a never-before-seen rate to keep up with the Fourth Industrial Revolution and current energy transition.

We hire outstanding people and give them a digital-first workplace and work/life flexibility to succeed. Our people-first culture, on-the-job training, and industry-leading benefits set employees prepared for success. Come join us and you might be behind the work we do, the challenges we address, and the mark we make on the industry.
