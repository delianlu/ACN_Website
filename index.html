ACN COLLABORATES WITH CLIENTS TO SOLVE COMPLEX CHALLENGES AND DEVELOP LEGACIES

ACN specializes in technically demanding engineering projects for a wide variety of sustainable design, high-rise buildings, general contracting, construction management, as well as renewable energy plants, power generation, and electrical transmission and distribution lines.

We offer a multidisciplinary approach and expertise to ensure the success of every project we run with our clients.

ABOUT

ACN Engineering Co. has been involved in technically demanding tasks for a wide variety of engineering projects since its establishment. ACN has a wealth of industry experience on a global scale in addition to the knowledge and experience of its management team. The main operating markets include of Turkey, the Gulf region, Eastern Europe, Sub-Saharan Africa, and Central and South Asia. The company is confident in its capacity to complete projects, including high-rise buildings, road and bridge construction projects, dry ports, railroads, hydroelectric power plants, industrial facilities, power distribution networks, water distribution systems and environmental projects.
